GRAPHICS

MOVIE POSTER

This project was to recreate a movie poster. I chose a classic movie, and decided to create a poster with a bit of a twist. I wanted the poster to represent the movie well, yet stand out from what you would normally picture in your mind. I chose to use the iconic rose from Beauty and the Beast, and have it feel magical. The poster was created using Adobe Photoshop and Indesign.

7 STORIES PLAY POSTER

Winning poster design for the Algonquin College Theatre department. The college held a contest to design a poster for their upcoming play titled 7 Stories. The client described her vision for the poster, and the basis of the play. To create this poster I began sketching out different concepts. After deciding which concept to go with, I then scanned my artwork to the computer. The final piece was created in Adobe Illustrator using the pen tool and shapes. The typography for the title was chosen to match the quirkiness of the play, and the additional information was set in a complimentary font that is easily legible.

WEBSITE GRAPHICS

These graphics were created for different sections of a website. The colours chosen remained in theme with the website itself. Each graphic was created through research and sketching, and then finalized in Adobe Illustrator.
