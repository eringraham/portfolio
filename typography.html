TYPOGRAPHY

EDITORIAL LAYOUT

This is an editorial layout of an article that is about Audrey Hepburn. The article describes how Audrey is a fashion icon, and she shouldn’t be used in a commercial for the Gap. My aim was to keep the article in the high fashion theme. I mixed modern and cursive fonts, and kept a strong layout with ample whitespace. I kept the colours black and white for high contrast, and used a pop of a dark purple.

ARTICLE LAYOUT

This was a newsletter article for a home and garden magazine. I kept the design here bright and fresh with colours that appealed to the target audience and fit the tone of the article. The fonts were a mid of a serif for the body, and a cursive font for the accented text.

2 PAGE SPREAD

This article is a 2 page magazine spread. The article is about Britney Spears’ show coming to Las Vegas. I chose colours to accent the photographs for the article, and really make the piece pop. The typography was kept clean and bold, with a sans serif for the headlines, and serif for the body copy. I wanted the design to be bold and representative of Britney.
